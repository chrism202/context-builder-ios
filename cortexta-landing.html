How Cortexta works

1 · Capture

Use the iOS share sheet or quick jot. Cortexta grabs the text, link, and image context instantly.

2 · Organise

We auto-cluster items into scopes like “Dev Preferences” or “Trip to Tokyo”. Rename, merge, or pin as needed.

3 · Attach

Pick a scope when you start a chat. Your assistant reads the brief, stays on-topic, and forgets it afterwards.

Private by default

Everything lives on-device with optional iCloud sync. Export in Markdown, JSON, or ZIP anytime.

Human-paced nudges

Opt into gentle reminders for goals you’ve saved. No spam, no notifications you didn’t ask for.

Built for speed

Zero required fields, instant undo, and search that spans highlights plus OCR text.
